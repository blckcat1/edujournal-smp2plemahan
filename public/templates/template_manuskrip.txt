# EduJournal Manuscript Writing Template

Use this template to write your research paper for EduJournal SMP/SMA.

## Sections:
1. Title (Judul)
2. Author Name & Class (Nama Siswa & Kelas)
3. School Name (Nama Sekolah/Institusi)
4. Abstract (Abstrak) - max 200 words
5. Keywords (Kata Kunci) - 3 to 5 words
6. Introduction (Pendahuluan)
7. Methods (Metode Penelitian)
8. Results & Discussion (Hasil & Pembahasan)
9. Conclusion (Kesimpulan)
10. References (Daftar Pustaka) - APA Style
